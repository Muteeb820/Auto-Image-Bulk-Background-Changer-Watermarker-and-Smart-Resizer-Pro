Bulk E-Com Composer Pro

The Ultimate Python Engine for Automated Product Imagery, Background Replacement, and Dynamic Watermarking via Google Colab.

🌟 System Overview

Managing e-commerce visuals at scale is a massive challenge. This engine is designed to eliminate manual graphic design entirely. By strictly organizing your Google Drive into a specific folder architecture, this Python script uses the PIL (Python Imaging Library) to mathematically layer backgrounds, products, and watermarks.

It is specifically built to handle unlimited bulk processing, standardizing your entire catalog for Google Merchant Center in minutes.

⚙️ Core Capabilities Explained

1. Dynamic Layering Architecture

The system builds images like a digital sandwich. It places your chosen background at the bottom, centers your transparent product image in the middle, and stamps your watermark on the very top, preserving all alpha-transparency channels perfectly.

2. Smart Auto-Framing

You never have to worry about stretched or distorted images. The engine calculates the aspect ratio of each layer independently and uses high-quality `LANCZOS` resampling to fit them perfectly into your designated output canvas.

📁 The 4-Folder Architecture

To run this tool, you must create exactly these four folders in your Google Drive. The script reads and writes to these specific locations.

🏞️

1_background

Purpose: The Base Layer.
Place your solid colors, branded graphics, or aesthetic scenic backgrounds here. You can place as many backgrounds as you want to trigger the Batch Generator.

🛍️

2_product

Purpose: The Middle Layer.
Place your thousands of raw product images here. The script processes every single image in this folder.

©️

3_watermark

Purpose: The Top Layer.
Place exactly ONE brand logo here. It must be a transparent PNG file so it doesn't block the product underneath.

✨

4_output

Purpose: The Destination.
Do not manually put anything here. The script will automatically generate your final catalog inside this folder.

🔄 Multi-Background Generation Logic

This is the most powerful feature of the engine. It is designed to create multiple variations of your catalog instantly.

How The Loop Works:

  1. The engine looks inside 1_background and finds Background A and Background B.
  2. It creates a new folder in your output called /Background_A_Results.
  3. It takes all 500 products from Folder 2, merges them with Background A, and saves them.
  4. It then creates another folder called /Background_B_Results.
  5. It takes those same 500 products, merges them with Background B, and saves them.

Result: You just generated 1,000 unique creatives with one click.

🎛️ Colab UX & Custom Parameters

The Google Colab notebook features an interactive UI form, allowing you to tweak settings without touching the core Python code.

📏 Custom Canvas Size

Enter your exact Width and Height (e.g., 1080x1080 for Instagram/GMC, or 1200x800 for web banners). The script will standardize every single output image to this exact pixel count.

👻 Watermark Opacity Slider

A slider ranging from 0 to 100. If you set it to 30%, the engine mathematically reduces the Alpha Channel of your logo, making it transparent and subtle, ensuring the product remains the hero of the image.

💡 Pro-Tips & Fallback Mode

🎯 For Perfect Results (Transparent Products)

To fully utilize the Background Replacement feature, the images inside your 2_product folder must have their original backgrounds removed (saved as transparent PNGs). This allows the new background from Folder 1 to show through beautifully.

⚠️ Fallback Mode (Non-Transparent Products)

What if your product images still have their white/grey backgrounds?
The script won't crash! It will intelligently act as a Bulk Watermarker. It will ignore the background replacement (because your product's solid background covers the canvas) but it will still stamp your Watermark on top of all images and resize them perfectly.
